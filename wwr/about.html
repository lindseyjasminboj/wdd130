White Whater Rafting company

Our team members report a high sense of fulfillment derived from mastering the elements and leading others through challenges. The "office" is a breathtaking canyon, and the "meetings" are high-fives after a successful drop. This connection to nature and the tight-knit camaraderie among the crew creates a workplace where laughter is as constant as the current.

History

  • Ten years ago, a group of river guides led by a man named Elias founded White Water Rafting. Based in the heart of the Rocky Mountains, the company started with just three rafts and a passion for the Class IV rapids of the "The Pathway".

  • For years, the business was simple. Customers would walk up to the wooden shack by the river, sign a paper waiver, and pay in cash. Elias kept track of the schedule in a large, coffee-stained notebook. If the sun was shining and the water was high, it was a good day.
